Arrival

Enter through the entrance on University Avenue and follow the signs and volunteers to the check-in booth. If you are not a UT student, parking is available in the conference center garage.

Slack →

Make sure to join Slack before the event to make you stay up-to-date during the event. You can also use Slack to find teams, ask for help, or talk to others during workshops.

Company APIs

If you need inspiration for APIs to use in your projects, take a look at our sponsors' APIs:

  • MongoDB Stitch: applications on top of MongoDB
  • Citi: global finance API
  • Azure: trusted cloud designed for you
  • RevSpeech API: advanced speech recognition

Check-in

To expedite check-in, please have your QR code found in your email ready for scanning. If you arrive late, please head to the check-in desk and we will do our best to help get you started (note that entry is not guaranteed even if you confirmed).

Contact us

During the event you can reach out to us in person (the front desk should be staffed), send a message on Slack, or send an email to hello@freetailhackers.com.

Need a hand?

We will have mentors at the event who can help with anything regarding your project! You can get help with anything from how to get started or debugging some issue you run into by using the /help-ticket command in any channel on the event Slack.
