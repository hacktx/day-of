FAQs

What should I do before I come to the event?

Brainstorm some ideas and get a team together! We also recommend downloading our iOS or Android app and reading over the rest of this site.

What should I bring?

  • Yourself!
  • Your valid student ID (if in college) and a government-issued ID
  • Your computer and charger
  • Any other equipment you might need for your project (i.e. a drawing tablet)
  • A blanket and/or sweatshirt
  • Most of all, bring energy, ideas, and a great attitude so you can have tons of fun!
warning
Firearms, weapons, alcohol, illegal drugs, power tools, and the Samsung Galaxy Note 7 are not allowed.

Do I need a team?

You don’t need one, but we strongly recommend joining a team. You can post on our Facebook group for team members, or meet people at the beginning of the event. Teams can be 2-5 people! The more the merrier!

When and where do I check-in?

  • Check-in begins at 9:30 AM and will continue until the opening ceremony at 11 AM.
  • The check-in desk will be located in the hotel atrium.
  • Head to the check-in desk as soon as you arrive.
  • To expedite check-in, please download our app and use the check-in feature to get your QR code for scanning.
  • If you arrive after these aforementioned times, please head to the check-in desk and we will do our best to help get you started.

What do I need to check-in/what do I do after I check-in?

You'll need your student ID (if in college) and a government-issued ID. To expedite check-in, download our app and use the check-in feature to get your QR code for scanning. Head to the opening ceremony as soon as you check-in (we'll direct you)!

When can I work on my project?

You may work on your project from 12:00 PM (noon) on October 22nd until 12:00 PM (noon) on October 23rd. Please do not work on your project at other times as it will disqualify you from the judging rounds. Of course, this does not mean that what you make during HackTX cannot be the start of a bigger project to complete with your group, the hacker community, or on your own.

How do I submit my project?

All projects will be submitted through DevPost at the end of the event. We will let you know 1 hour, 30 minutes, and finally 15 minutes before all projects are due. We will provide more details towards the end of the event when it is more pertinent.

What can I work on?

You can work on anything that uses technology! This can be a website, mobile app, a video game, a VR/AR app, etc. Use your imagination to come up with a unique tech project!

It’s my first time, what should I expect?

You should expect to have fun! Expect to learn, meet new friends, and enjoy a great environment and community driven to helping each other! Learn from your peers and mentors. We are here to help!

How do I get help from a mentor?

There will be mentors walking around your workspace, so just call them out if you need help! You can also go the the help desk or use our mentor site.

What if I have a question that wasn’t answered from above?

If you have a question before the event, please email hello@hacktx.com.

If you have a question during the event, a help desk will be located in the atrium near the Capital Ballroom. Otherwise, you can find an organizer or director who will be wearing an organizer shirt and they will answer your question or direct you to someone who can.
