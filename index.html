Arrival

Enter through the entrance on University Avenue and follow the signs and volunteers to the check-in booth. If you are not a UT student, parking is available in the conference center garage.

Check-in

To expedite check-in, please download our app and use the check-in feature to get your QR code for scanning. You can also get the latest news and see the full schedule using our iOS and Android apps. If you arrive late, please head to the check-in desk and we will do our best to help get you started.
